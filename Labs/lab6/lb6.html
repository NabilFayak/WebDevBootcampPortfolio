Favicons!

What exactly is a favicon?

A favicon could be classified almost like a website's logo. It's the icon that is shown on the website tab.

How are favicons created?

Favicons are created by many lines of code, many bits that each represents the color for a pixel shown. The colors in the favicon are represented by a combination of 1s and 0s.

How did I create the favicon for this site?
